who i am

I am driven by a passion for crafting seamless and impactful experiences. Proficient in administrative tasks and well-versed in providing quality service to customers.

Little about me

AJ is fueled by her passion for exploring cultural diversity. She pursued a Bachelor of Science in Tourism Management at one of Manila, Philippines' top and most respected universities. During her studies, she completed an internship at a government agency focused on culture, where she honed her administrative skills and played a pivotal role in assessing Local Cultural Inventory.

After graduating, She dedicated herself to customer relationship management in the healthcare industry, collaborating with leading HMO providers and BPO clients. It was during this period that she discovered the world of short-term rental property management, realizing that she could apply her knowledge and experience to fulfill her passion for providing memorable experiences.

With unwavering determination, she made a concerted effort to learn property management systems, ultimately securing a position as a property management assistant. In this role, she contributed significantly to efficient operations and enhanced guest experiences.

AJ firmly believes that understanding human experiences is paramount for business success. She derives immense satisfaction from using her skill set to empower individuals to achieve their goals.

WHAT I DO FOR FUN?

In her leisure time, AJ enjoys traveling and has explored destinations such as Singapore and Thailand. She relishes spending quality moments with her five dogs and cherishing precious time with her family. AJ is also looking forward to learning pottery and continuing to explore new skills and talents to enrich her life.
